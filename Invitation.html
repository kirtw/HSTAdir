Dear HSTAer's: It's time to get your request in to play next Sunday, 4pm-6pm. The courts fill up quickly many weeks.

Reply to this email with one of these choices:

1)  Im in.
2)  I regrettably cannot this week.
3)  I am not sure yet- keep me on the waiting list.

Directors for this month:
@name@ @opt phone here@

If your email address is obscure, then sign your name in the body

BTW, you do not need to edit the subject as requested in days past.
