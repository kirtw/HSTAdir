Subjects of emails -  tldr

    Home   Invitation   

To keep my life in control, monitoring all these emails, please follow the following guidelines-

Start the Subject with HSTA  of all ResHSTA emails.
Next Use pretty standard terminology-
    1) Invitation
    2) You're In
    3)  Dinner at
    4)  More Players Needed

Put the date (and year!) as the third part of the Subject, but do NOT repeat it in the body.
Repeating it just allows for errors, trust me.

Send a separate dinner email to the whole Invitation-List so those not playing or not in may still easily join us.
Also, no one reads past the subject !
I (and everyone) can see the Subject without having to open the email.

I love the abbreviation tldr  - Too Long Didn't Read
